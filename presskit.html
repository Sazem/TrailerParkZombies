Description

Trailer Park Zombies is a survival roguelite shooter with random procedural levels.
Developt by one developer.

The game was inspirated by boomer shooters like original Doom, DukeNukem 3D and
modern roquelites like Vampire Survival. Game is challenging. Each round lasts usually few minutes. To win, you have to survive 15 minutes.

Trailer Park Zombies has 90s asthetics with lowpoly 3d models and pixel art textures & sprites.

Features

  • Randomly Generated Levels
  • Destructible environment
  • 1990s visuals
  • 3 Playable characters
